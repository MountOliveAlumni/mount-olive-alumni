🏫 Our Story

The Mount Olive Alumni Portal was created to bridge the gap between former students, associates, and executives of Mount Olive Good Shepherd. Focusing on the foundational years of 2013–2019, this platform serves as a digital home for those who walked the halls and learned under the guidance of "The Generals."

🎯 Mission & Vision

Our Mission: To build a strong, active community that supports former students academically, socially, and professionally.

Our Vision: To become a global network that enables collaboration, mentorship, and lifelong relationships between all Mount Olive graduates.
