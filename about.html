🏫 About Mount Olive Alumni Website

This is the official Mount Olive Alumni Website created to connect former students, associates, and executives from different graduating years (2013–2019).

🎯 Purpose of the Website

  • Connect past students from 2013–2019
  • Preserve student records and achievements
  • Help alumni stay in touch
  • Create a digital school community

🎯 Mission

To build a strong, connected and active alumni community that supports former students academically, socially, and professionally while preserving their achievements and growth.

🌍 Vision

To become a fully digital alumni network platform that connects all graduates of Mount Olive Good Shepherd worldwide, enabling networking, collaboration, mentorship, and lifelong relationships.

👥 What the Website Contains

  • Home page
  • Class of 2013–2019 students
  • Associates section
  • Executives section
  • Student profiles
  • Alumni Contacts

💡 Features

  • Search for students
  • View alumni by year
  • See executive members
  • Easy navigation system
