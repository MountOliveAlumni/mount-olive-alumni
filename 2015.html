About the Class of 2015

The Class of 2015 is remembered for discipline, unity, and academic excellence at Mount Olive Good Shepherd Primary School. Students excelled in academics, sports, and co-curricular activities, building a strong bond that remains today. Many are now pursuing professional careers and higher education worldwide.
