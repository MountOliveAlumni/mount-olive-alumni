Class of 2015

A Year of Excellence, Discipline & Great Memories

About the Class of 2015

The Class of 2015 at Mount Olive Good Shepherd Primary School in Gulu, Uganda, is remembered for discipline, unity, and academic excellence. The students built strong foundations that shaped their future success.

They participated in class tests, mid-term exams, and final examinations, along with sports, Music Dance and Drama (MDD), group discussions, and school duties.

Many members of this class are now pursuing education and careers in different parts of the world.
