Class of 2017

The UNEB Pioneers & Academic Leaders

Pioneering Excellence

The Class of 2017 stands as one of the most significant cohorts in the history of Mount Olive Good Shepherd. As the first class to use UNEB Centre Numbers, they set the standard for academic rigor and discipline. This year also marked a legendary transition, celebrated by the most memorable farewell in school history for the mentors who built the foundation of our success.
