About the Class of 2017

The Class of 2017 at Mount Olive Good Shepherd Primary School was one of the most historic classes. It marked the year the school officially used the UNEB examination center number.

The class was known for strong competition, discipline, leadership, and teamwork in academics and activities.

A special leadership system was introduced where Moses led Mathematics and Opiyo led Science, both performing consistently throughout the year.

This year also marked the farewell of beloved teachers: Tr. Paul, Tr. Anthony, Tr. Charles, and Tr. Robert.
