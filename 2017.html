About the Class of 2017

The Class of 2017 was one of the strongest and most disciplined classes in the history of Mount Olive Good Shepherd. It marked an important academic year with strong performance, leadership, and unity. The 2017 cohort was the first to use UNEB Centre Numbers at the school. It also marked the end of great teachers’ service and the most memorable farewell in the school’s history, honoring dedicated teachers such as Mr. Paul, Mr. Charles, Mr. Anthony, and Mr. Robert, who played a major role in shaping the students’ success
