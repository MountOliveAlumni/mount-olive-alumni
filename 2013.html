Class of 2013

Foundation of Excellence, Discipline & Unity

Academic Pioneers

The Class of 2013 holds a special place in Mount Olive history as the first-ever candidate class. It marked the beginning of national examinations and established a strong foundation for academic excellence. This pioneering class is remembered for discipline and determination, setting a high standard for all who followed.
