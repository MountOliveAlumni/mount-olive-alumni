About the Class of 2013

The Class of 2013 holds a special place in Mount Olive history as the first-ever candidate class. It marked the beginning of national examinations and established a strong foundation for academic excellence.

This pioneering class is remembered for discipline, unity, and determination, setting a high standard for future generations.

Today, its members are contributing across various sectors including law, medicine, business, engineering, and public service.
