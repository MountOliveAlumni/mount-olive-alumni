About the Class of 2014

The Class of 2014 is remembered as one of the most disciplined and united groups in Mount Olive Good Shepherd Primary School.

They demonstrated strong academic excellence, leadership, and teamwork throughout their school journey.

The class actively participated in academics, sports, and co-curricular activities, building a strong foundation for future success.

Their bond extended beyond the classroom, creating lifelong friendships and mutual support among classmates.

Today, members of the Class of 2014 are excelling in various fields including education, healthcare, business, security services, and international careers.

The legacy of this class continues to inspire younger generations through discipline, determination, and hard work.
