About the Class of 2016

The Class of 2016 at Mount Olive Good Shepherd Primary School was a hardworking and disciplined group. They participated in academics, sports, Music Dance and Drama (MDD), debates, and school activities.

The class is remembered for unity, teamwork, and respect for teachers. Many students continued to achieve success in different fields.
